\begin{center}
$x*y = \sqrt{xy}$, for all $x,y\in\Q^{+}$.
\end{center}

\begin{center}
$U_{n} = \SET{z\in\C:z^n = 1}$
\end{center}
be the set of all $n$th roots of unity.  Prove that $U_{n}$ is a binary algebraic structure under complex multiplication.
